Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Pookie collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Partner connection data
  • Messages and doodles shared with your partner
  • App preferences and customizations

We automatically collect:

  • Device information and identifiers
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Connect you with your partner
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Process purchases and subscriptions
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Your connected partner (messages, activities)
  • Service providers who help operate our app
  • Law enforcement when required by law

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Pookie is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@pookieapp.com
